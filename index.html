Welcome to Sean's Tree Dichotomous Key

First, we need to understand what a dichotomous key is!

A dicatomous key is a tool used to identify different plants and wildlife. You are given simple true or false, yes or no type questions in a manner that leads you to a specific answer.

For example, we could be proposed a question like "Is the leaf simple or compound?" and from there go further into detail on what tree that leaf belongs to.
